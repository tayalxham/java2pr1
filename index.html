Book of the Month: Not Even Bones

Want to read with us?

Not even Bones is a a thriller novel and a story with a refreshing and unique setting. It's a beautiful mix of fantasy and horror and presents the question to the reader 'How far is too far?' When you have to choose between yourself and others what lines will you cross? What are you willing to do to ensure your own survival and at what point do you become the villian?
